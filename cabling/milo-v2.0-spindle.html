Milo V2.0 Spindle Power and Control

Diagram

Milo V2.0 provides single phase 110v or 220v AC power to a 48v PSU and a VFD,
for spindle control. The AC power to the VFD is controlled by a 3-pole contactor,
which is triggered by an Emergency Stop circuit. The Spindle is directly connected
to the VFD and grounded, with a cable shield for noise reduction.
