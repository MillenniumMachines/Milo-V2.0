Milo V2.0 DC Power

Diagram

Milo V2.0 provides 48v DC power via a 110-220v to 48v PSU. Power is
distributed to the mainboard, directly for the MCU itself, and via the
3rd pole of a contactor for powering the motors. The contactor is
triggered by an Emergency Stop circuit.
