Milo V2.0 Emergency Stop

Diagram

Milo V2.0 utilises 2 emergency stop circuits, implemented using 2 normally closed switches on each emergency stop button. The first circuit is used to trigger the contactor, which cuts power to the VFD and motors. The second circuit is used to send an emergency stop signal to the mainboard, which triggers a stop or pause in RepRapFirmware and allows the operator to investigate before rebooting.
