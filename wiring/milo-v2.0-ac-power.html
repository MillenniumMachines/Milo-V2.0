Milo V2.0 AC Power

Diagram

Milo V2.0 provides single phase 110v or 220v AC power to a 24v PSU, a 48v PSU and
a VFD, for spindle control. The AC power to the VFD and 48v PSU is controlled by
a contactor, which is triggered by an Emergency Stop circuit. The Spindle is
directly connected to the VFD and grounded, with a cable shield for noise reduction.
